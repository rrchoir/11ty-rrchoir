We will sing the following verses of We Are Marching:\
\
We are marching in the light of Truth...\
Caminando por la verdad...\
We are singing for liberty...\
Cantando por la libertad...

\
We will sing the following verses for This Little Light:

This little light of mine...\
For Alex and Renee,,,\
All across our country,,,\
For love and dignity,,,\
This little light of mine...

As the crowd disperses, we will sing Hold On by Heidi Wilson: \
 Lyrics: Hold on, hold on, my dear ones, here comes the dawn.
