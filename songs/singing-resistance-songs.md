This is a collection of Singing Resistance Songs that the RRC sings. Links to videos and audio clips are below the lyrics to all the songs.

**I Send My Hope**  by Jordan Mudd\
I send my hope. (Echo)\
I send my prayer. (Echo)\
I send my strength to those resisting everywhere.

May you stand tall. (Echo)\
May you stay true. (Echo)\
May you walk forward towards the work you're called to do.

\
**No One is Getting Left Behind** (We Get There Together) by Joshua Blaine\
No one is getting left behind this time.\
No one is getting left behind.\
No one is getting left behind this time.\
We get there together, or never get there at all. (3x)

\
**This is for Our Neighbors** by Lu Aya of the Peace Poets \
This is for our neighbors who are locked inside.\
Together, we will, abolish ICE.

Zipper words instead of "neighbors": people, families...

\
**We Are Here** by Sarina Partridge\
We are here. (Echo)\
In our city. (Echo)\
Our love for each other will carry us through.\
Yes, our love for each other will carry us through.

Zipper words instead of city: courage, families, children, demands, voices, neighbors, anger, singing, In our grief, In our hope

\
**We Are Many** by Katie Eckeberger\
We are many. (Echo)\
We are one. (Echo)\
We won't stop fighting\
'Til ICE is gone!

We are many. (Echo)\
We are one. (Echo)\
The fight ain't over\
'Til love has won!

\
**We Belong to Each Other** by Annie Schlaefer, inspired by a poem by Nikita Gill\
Everyone, oh everyone, oh everyone of these people are ours,\
just like we are theirs.\
We belong to them, and they belong to us.

\
**We Don't Want No Camps** by Amanda\
We don't want no camps. (3x)\
You can stop this violence.\
We don't want no camps\
You can stop this nonsense.\
We don't want no camps.
